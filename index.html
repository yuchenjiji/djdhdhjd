Popular Tutorial

The Art of Smoky Eyes – Step by Step

Master the iconic smoky eye look with our easy-to-follow tutorial. This timeless style adds depth and drama, perfect for an evening out or any occasion where you want to make a bold statement. Let's dive into the steps to achieve that perfect smoky eye.

What You'll Need:

  • Pedro's Ultimate Eye Shadow Collection
  • Smooth Eyeliner Pencil (Preferably in dark shades like black or deep brown)
  • Mascara (Preferably volumizing)
  • Makeup Brushes (Blending brush and a smudge brush)

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Prep Your Lids: Start with a clean and primed eyelid for the shadow to adhere properly.
  2. Base Color: Apply a light, neutral color from the Ultimate Eye Shadow Collection over your entire eyelid as the base.
  3. Define the Crease: Use a slightly darker shade to define your crease, blending well for a seamless transition.
  4. Smoky Effect: Apply a dark shadow (like charcoal or dark brown) along your eyelid and into the crease. Blend upwards and outwards. The key is blending; you want no harsh lines.
  5. Eyeliner: Line your upper and lower lash lines with the Smooth Eyeliner Pencil. For a more intense look, line your waterline.
  6. Smudge It Up: Use a smudge brush to smudge the eyeliner on both upper and lower lash lines. This step adds to the smoky effect.
  7. Highlight: Apply a shimmery highlight shade to your brow bone and the inner corner of your eyes.
  8. Mascara: Finish off with a coat or two of volumizing mascara to make your lashes stand out.

And there you have it – a gorgeous smokey eye look that’s perfect for any glamorous occasion. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t hesitate to experiment with different shades and intensities from our Ultimate Eye Shadow Collection.
